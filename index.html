Welcome to LoL Bets

How does LoL Bets work?
You will be placing bets based on League of Legends streams. Depending on the bet type you have betted on will determine if you have won the bet. If you have won your bet you will be paid at the end of the event. Please allow up to 10 minutes to 24 hours for your payments to be sent to you.

What can I bet on?
  1. First Kill - Place bets on if the streamer will get 1st kill.
  2. Team Win - Place bets based on which team you will think is going to win. The enemy team or streamers team.
  3. Lane Win - Will the streamer win lane?
  4. Champion KDA prediction - Predict how many kills/deaths/assist they will get

Placing your bets
To place a bet just send your funds to the responding addresses (are visible for public to view). Once you have paid contact me directly or send a email to admin@btc-bets.com with your payment transaction and your bet prediction. Once verified and confirme your listing will be added to the pool.
